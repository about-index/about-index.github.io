Super Smash Bros. (Nintendo 64)
DESCRIPTION

Super Smash Bros is a fighting game that features characters from various Nintendo franchises, such as Mario, Zelda, and Pokemon. Players can choose from a roster of characters and battle against each other in iconic Nintendo settings. The game's unique gameplay mechanics, such as the ability to knock opponents off the stage, set it apart from traditional fighting games. It was released in 1999, was the first game of the Super Smash Bros. series, and is considered a classic among Nintendo fans.
