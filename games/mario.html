Mario 64
DESCRIPTION

Mario 64 is a classic 3D platform game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo 64 console. It was released in 1996 and is widely considered one of the greatest video games of all time. The game features Mario as the main protagonist, who must navigate a series of levels and collect power stars to rescue Princess Peach from the clutches of the nefarious Bowser. The game introduces many new gameplay elements, such as a free-roaming 3D environment, and is known for its revolutionary camera system and tight controls. Mario 64 is a must-play for any fan of platformers and a true classic of the genre.
